Empowering Women. Strengthening Communities.

RESEAU DE DEVELOPMENT DES FEMMES PAUVRES(RDFP) works to uplift women, youth and families from poor households through education, capacity building, advocacy, and community programs. Founded in 2010 and led by Crescence Mukantabana—an activist for women's rights since 1986—PWDN is a trusted voice for gender equality and economic resilience.

About RDFP

Founded: 2010
Founder & Executive Director: Crescence Mukantabana — a woman champion, activist, and feminist advocate, coach, mentor on gender equality, women's empowerment, and human rights since 1986.

RDFP is community-driven — focusing on dignity, rights, and sustainable solutions for women and youth in Rwanda.

Vision

To have a Rwandan society where all women enjoy their rights.

Mission

To contribute to empowering women in social justice, politics, economics, health, gender equality, prevent all forms of gender based violence, construction of peace culture, climate change towards her sustainable development.

Core Values

Intervention Strategies

Capacity Building

Training and mentorship programs to develop skills, entrepreneurship and leadership for women, girls, boys,children, and household.

Education / IEC

Information, education and communication on rights, health, economic opportunities and civic participation.

Research

Local research to inform policy, program design and targeted interventions for vulnerable communities.

Advocacy & Lobby

Lobbying for gender-sensitive policies, legal protection and access to resources for women.

Groups & Cooperatives

Organization and formation of groups/cooperatives to strengthen savings, access to microfinance and markets.

Technical & Financial Support

Providing small grants, technical assistance and linkages to partners to scale impact.

Programs & Success Stories

Suistainable development

Capacity building for women in climate change, environment protection, resilience, and unpaid care-works. Capacity building for women in agro-ecology and smart agriculture. Capacity building for women in Farming and provision of animals for farming. Equal distribution of existence resources. Capacity buildind of women and income generating acivities(Entrepreneurship, VSLS,Job creation)

Social justice

Training of 300 women in economic empowerement, gender equality, GBV prevention and laws in Nyarugenge District, Gasabo District,and Muhanga District.

Peace building

Capacity building of 300 women and 1325 resolution (Women peace and security)translated into Kinyarwanda in District Nyarugenge District, Gasabo District, and Muhanga District.

Health

Creation of advocacy community movement of adolescent and 300 young women in sexual reproductive health and right in Nyarugenge District (Kimisagara), Gasabo District(Ngara), and Hanika school located in Muhanga District. Capacity building of women on desease prevention and Tabacco control. Dismination of information concerning prevention and mitigation of COVID-19 advocacy and awareness compaign on sexual reproductive health, GBV prevention, and gender equality.
